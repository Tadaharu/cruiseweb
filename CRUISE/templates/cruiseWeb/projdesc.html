What is it?

CRUISE is a traffic reporting system that gathers information through crowdsourcing. CRUISE crawls traffic related data from Twitter and processes it into useful data which could be useful to the users.


Motivation

Enclosed vehicles have applications like Waze, Google Maps, which allows them can avoid traffic congestion. Howeverm, semi-enclosed vehicles doesnâ€™t have such luxury. Using these apps will rob them of their attention. Thus this project conveys a way to transmit the traffic data to the motorcyclists through crowd-sourcing through Twiter or the app users (PHASE 2).


How it works

CRUISE crawls and filters the traffic related tweets from Twitter. The tweets are then passed on to undergo a series of NLP processes to obtain the traffic location and the traffic states. After that, contextual analysis is carried out to relate the relationships between the locations and traffic state. Reconciliation is then carried out to conclude the actual traffic states of respective locations and then stored into the database.


How to obtain it

It's easy ! Just install the CRUISE app and it will report the traffic conditions around* you (depending on the sensitivity set). Currently the app is on closed beta, it will be available for public soon !
